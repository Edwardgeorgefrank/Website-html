BBC ​PALE ALE

 

SESSION PACIFIC PALE ALE

BBC Pacific Pale Ale pours light copper with a bright white head, this distinctly New Zealand Pale Ale deliver a nose of sweet tropical fruit and resinous pine.

 

RAWENE

 

Coconut & Vanilla Oatmeal Stout

Deep brown with a tight crema-like head, Rawene oozes smoothness and complexity. Initial chocolate malt aroma gives way to a delicate blend of esters, with notes of vanilla pod and subtle coconut sweetness. Body is voluptuous, with hop flavour and bitterness restrained to ensure drinkability.

BBC ​PILSNER

 

GATEWAY PILSNER

Our New Zealand Pilsner pours golden clear with aromas of passionfruit, citrus and spice. A cleansing bitterness from local hops adds a perfect finish.
